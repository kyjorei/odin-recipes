Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Process

  1. Put raw chicken in bowl.
  2. Add white vinegar and wash chicken in vinegar.
  3. Rise and repeat.
  4. Drain vinegar and water then add salt and pepper to chicken.
  5. Heat up pot or pan.
  6. Slap chicken in that pot or pan.
  7. Let it cook.
  8. You did it!
