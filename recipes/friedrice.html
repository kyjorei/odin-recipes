Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Peas
  • Carrots
  • Soy Sauce
  • Scallion
  • Egg

Process

  1. Heat up your pan, add oil, let oil get hot.
  2. Add cooked rice.
  3. Add veggies and toss veggies + rice in hot oil.
  4. Move cooked rice and veggies to side of pan, then add egg.
  5. Toss egg, veggies, rice, until eggs cooked through.
  6. Lower heat, add soy sauce, and continue tossing fried rice.
  7. Turn off heat. Plate and serve.
